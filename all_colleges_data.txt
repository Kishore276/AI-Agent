## Faq

**Frequently Asked Questions:**
  **Admission Related:**
    **Question:** What is the eligibility criteria for admission to Anna University?
    **Answer:** Candidates must qualify JEE Main and have 10+2 with PCM with minimum 60% marks.
    **Question:** What is the cutoff rank for Anna University?
    **Answer:** Cutoff ranks vary by branch and category. For CSE, the general category cutoff is typically around 20000.
    **Question:** What documents are required for admission?
    **Answer:** 10th & 12th certificates, entrance exam scorecard, category certificate (if applicable), and other standard documents.
  **Fee Related:**
    **Question:** What is the fee structure at Anna University?
    **Answer:** The annual fee is approximately INR 1.5-3 lakhs depending on the program including hostel and mess charges.
    **Question:** Are scholarships available?
    **Answer:** Yes, merit-based and need-based scholarships are available. SC/ST students get fee concessions as per government norms.
  **Placement Related:**
    **Question:** What is the placement record of Anna University?
    **Answer:** The college has excellent placement record with 85-90% placement rate and top companies visiting campus.
    **Question:** Which companies visit for placements?
    **Answer:** Top companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and many Fortune 500 companies visit for placements.
  **General Queries:**
    **Question:** Where is Anna University located?
    **Answer:** Anna University is located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.
    **Question:** What facilities are available on campus?
    **Answer:** The campus has excellent academic facilities, hostels, sports complex, medical center, and all modern amenities.

    **Answer:** JEE Advanced registration starts in April, exam in May, results in June, and JoSAA counseling from June onwards.

    **Question:** What is the total fee for B.Tech at IIT Bombay?
    **Answer:** The total fee is approximately INR 2,50,000 per year including tuition fee, hostel fee, and mess charges.
    **Question:** Are there any scholarships available?
    **Answer:** Yes, merit-cum-means scholarships, SC/ST scholarships, and various government scholarships are available. Institute also provides financial assistance to needy students.
    **Question:** Can I get an education loan?
    **Answer:** Yes, all major banks provide education loans for IIT students. The institute provides necessary documentation and has tie-ups with several banks.
    **Question:** What is the fee structure for international students?
    **Answer:** International students pay higher fees. DASA candidates pay approximately USD 3000 per semester. Self-financed international students pay higher fees.
    **Question:** Is there any fee concession for economically weaker sections?
    **Answer:** Yes, students from families with annual income below 5 lakhs get complete fee waiver. Income between 5-9 lakhs get 2/3rd fee concession.
  **Hostel Related:**
    **Question:** Is hostel accommodation guaranteed?
    **Answer:** Yes, hostel accommodation is guaranteed for all undergraduate students. Postgraduate students get accommodation based on availability.
    **Question:** What are the hostel facilities?
    **Answer:** Hostels have 24x7 Wi-Fi, laundry service, common rooms, reading rooms, gym, indoor games, mess facility, and 24x7 security.
    **Question:** Can I choose my hostel?
    **Answer:** Hostel allotment is done by the institute based on availability. Students can request preferences but final allotment depends on availability.

    **Answer:** Both vegetarian and non-vegetarian food is served. Special arrangements for Jain food and regional cuisines are available.

    **Answer:** Yes, parents can visit during specified visiting hours with prior permission from hostel authorities.

    **Question:** What is the medium of instruction?
    **Answer:** The medium of instruction for all courses is English.

    **Answer:** IIT Bombay follows a 10-point grading system with letter grades from AA (10) to FF (0).
    **Question:** Are there research opportunities for undergraduates?
    **Answer:** Yes, students can participate in research projects under faculty guidance through UROP (Undergraduate Research Opportunity Program) from the second year.

## Faq

**Frequently Asked Questions:**
  **Admission Related:**
    **Question:** What is the eligibility criteria for B.Tech admission?
    **Answer:** Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics with a minimum of 60% aggregate marks. Age limit is 17-25 years.
    **Question:** What entrance exams are accepted for admission?
    **Answer:** We accept KUEE (Kalasalingam University Entrance Exam), JEE Main, and TNEA scores for admission to various programs.
    **Question:** Is there a management quota for admission?
    **Answer:** Yes, 15% of seats are available under management quota. Eligibility criteria remain the same as regular admission. Contact +91-4626-251777 for details.
    **Question:** What is the cutoff rank for CSE in KUEE?
    **Answer:** The cutoff varies each year. For 2024, the closing rank for CSE was around 2500 in Phase 1. Phase 2 cutoffs are generally higher.
    **Question:** Can I get direct admission without entrance exam?
    **Answer:** Direct admission is available under management quota based on 12th standard marks and personal interview. Contact admissions office for details.
    **Question:** What documents are required for admission?
    **Answer:** 10th & 12th mark sheets, transfer certificate, conduct certificate, passport photos, Aadhar card, caste certificate (if applicable), and income certificate for scholarships.
  **Fee Related:**
    **Question:** What is the total fee for B.Tech first year?
    **Answer:** The total first year fee for B.Tech is INR 2,00,000 including tuition fee (1,60,000) and other fees (40,000). Hostel fees are separate.
    **Question:** Can I pay fees in installments?
    **Answer:** Yes, fees can be paid in two installments - 50% at admission and 50% before the second semester.
    **Question:** Are there any scholarships available?
    **Answer:** Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships (up to 50% fee waiver), need-based assistance (up to 75% concession), and category-based scholarships as per government norms.
    **Question:** What is the refund policy?
    **Answer:** 100% refund before admission (minus processing fee), 90% within 15 days, 75% within 30 days, and no refund after 30 days.
    **Question:** Can I get an education loan?
    **Answer:** Yes, the university provides all necessary documentation for education loans from nationalized and private banks. We have tie-ups with major banks.
  **Hostel Related:**
    **Question:** What are the hostel fees?
    **Answer:** Non-AC triple sharing: INR 1,10,000/year, AC triple sharing: INR 1,35,000/year (including mess fees of INR 45,000).
    **Question:** What facilities are available in hostels?
    **Answer:** 24x7 Wi-Fi, laundry service, common room with TV, reading room, gym, indoor games, 24x7 security, and CCTV surveillance.
    **Question:** Is hostel accommodation mandatory?
    **Answer:** Hostel accommodation is not mandatory, but highly recommended for outstation students. Local students can opt for day scholar.
    **Question:** What type of food is served in mess?
    **Answer:** We serve vegetarian, non-vegetarian, and Jain food. The mess operates from 7 AM to 10 PM with breakfast, lunch, snacks, and dinner.
    **Question:** Can parents visit hostels?
    **Answer:** Yes, parents can visit during specified visiting hours with prior permission from the hostel warden.
  **Academic Related:**
    **Question:** What is the medium of instruction?
    **Answer:** The medium of instruction for all engineering and science courses is English.
    **Question:** What specializations are available in CSE?
    **Answer:** AI & Machine Learning, Data Science, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing, Internet of Things, and Blockchain Technology.
    **Question:** Are there research opportunities for undergraduates?
    **Answer:** Yes, students can participate in research projects under faculty guidance from the second year onwards.
    **Question:** What is the attendance requirement?
    **Answer:** Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory for all courses. Students with less than 75% attendance may not be allowed to appear for exams.
    **Question:** Are there exchange programs with foreign universities?
    **Answer:** Yes, we have student exchange programs with universities in USA, Germany, and other countries for eligible students.
  **Placement Related:**
    **Question:** What is the placement percentage?
    **Answer:** Our overall placement percentage is 92% with CSE having 98% placement rate. The highest package offered was 45 LPA.
    **Question:** Which companies visit for placements?
    **Answer:** Top companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, TCS, Infosys, Wipro, L&T, and many more visit our campus for placements.
    **Question:** What is the average package offered?
    **Answer:** The average package is 8.5 LPA across all branches, with CSE having an average of 12.5 LPA.
    **Question:** Is there placement training provided?
    **Answer:** Yes, we provide comprehensive placement training including aptitude, technical skills, soft skills, and interview preparation.
    **Question:** Can students appear for off-campus placements?
    **Answer:** Yes, students are allowed to appear for off-campus placements along with on-campus opportunities.

    **Answer:** The campus is well-connected with 25 bus routes covering major cities. Nearest railway station is Virudhunagar (15 km) and airport is Madurai (80 km).
    **Question:** What are the library facilities?
    **Answer:** Our central library has 2.5 lakh books, 1 lakh e-books, 500 journals, and digital resources. It operates 24x7 with 800 seating capacity.

    **Answer:** Yes, we have cricket grounds, football field, basketball courts, tennis courts, swimming pool, gymnasium, and indoor stadium.
